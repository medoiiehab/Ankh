CHALLENGE 06:

Smart Recliner Chair

Reclining mechanism: Allows for various seating positions.
Built-in massage system: Provides customizable massage experiences.
Wireless headphones: For immersive audio experiences.
Wireless charging: For convenient charging of mobile devices.



The Challenge: Your task is to design and develop a mobile application that seamlessly interacts with the recliner chair, allowing users to:

Control the reclining mechanism: Adjust the chair to their preferred position.
Customize the massage experience: Select different massage modes, intensities, and durations.
Connect and control wireless headphones: Adjust volume, play/pause, and skip tracks.
Utilize the wireless charger: Monitor the charging status of their devices.
Rechargeable Recliner : Integrating a battery into the chair.



Key Features and Requirements:
User-friendly interface: The app should be intuitive and easy to navigate.
Customization options: Allow users to create and save their preferred chair settings (e.g., favorite reclining position, massage routine).
Bluetooth connectivity: Ensure a stable connection between the app and the chair.
Real-time control: Provide immediate response to user commands.
Battery level indicator: Display the battery status of the chair and connected devices.
Security features: Implement measures to protect user data and prevent unauthorized access.



Additional Considerations:
UtAccessibility: Consider users with disabilities and ensure the app is accessible to all.
Future scalability: Design the app with the potential to integrate additional features in the future.
Compatibility: Ensure compatibility with a wide range of mobile devices (iOS and Android).



Judging Criteria :
Functionality: How well the app meets the specified requirements.
User experience: The overall user-friendliness and intuitiveness of the app.
Innovation: Unique features or approaches that set the app apart.
Design: The visual appeal and aesthetics of the app.



Deliverables:
Working mobile application: A fully functional app that can be demonstrated on a mobile device.
Source code: All relevant code and documentation.
Presentation: A clear and concise presentation outlining the app's features and benefits..
