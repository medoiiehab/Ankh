F.A.Q

  • What is the "Ankh Innovation" hackathon?

    The "Ankh Innovation" Hackathon is a technical event aimed at meeting the challenges of the fourth industrial revolution (Industry 4.0). It is organized to bring together creators, developers and startups to develop innovative solutions using modern technology.

  • Who can participate in the hackathon?

    University students in the faculties of engineering, computers and science ,The team must contain a supervisor, either a university supervisor or a supervisor from the labor market .

  • How can I register for the hackathon ?

    You can register through the official website of the hackathon by filling out the registration form available on the "registration "page.

  • What challenges will be focused on at the hackathon?

    The focus will be on challenges such as smart industry

  • Can I get technical support during the hackathon?

    Yes, technical support teams will be available around the clock during the hackathon to help participants.

  • How will the projects be evaluated?

    The projects will be evaluated by a specialized jury based on criteria such as innovation, technical feasibility and applicability

  • How can I get more information?

    You can contact the organizing team via the "Contact Us" page on the site or send your inquiries via the dedicated e-mail.
