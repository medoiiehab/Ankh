About The Event

Hackathon "Ankh for innovation" aims to find innovative solutions to industrial problems using revolutionary technologies The Fourth Industrial, includes training sessions, workshops, technical support, evaluation by experts, with the provision of Prizes and certificates for participants and winners. The hackathon will be promoted via social media Cooperation with universities and companies.
